Tombstone Kane

A Wonderful Wizard Of Words

Tombstone Kane is a distinct voice in the literary landscape and an astute student of the titans of the past; most notably Dostoyevky, Hamsun, Joyce, Faulkner, Gaddis, Gass, and Pynchon, to name a few.

Tired of reading books that were little more than a copy of a copy of a copy, he set out to do something about it and write novels that clearly go beyond simple story telling and show a tremendous appreciation for the art of writing and fulfillment of a profound longing for originality and uniqueness. That's why there is nothing like a Tombstone Kane!
